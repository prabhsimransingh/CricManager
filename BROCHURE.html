What is CricManager?

CricManager is a FREE online cricket analytics platform that helps you analyze match data, track player performance, build balanced teams, and create professional match cards. No signup required, no tracking, and your data stays completely private on your device.

Why You'll Love It

  • ✓ Completely free and open source
  • ✓ No signup or registration needed
  • ✓ Works on any device (phone/tablet/desktop)
  • ✓ 14 pre-loaded matches (100+ players)
  • ✓ All data stays on YOUR device
  • ✓ Works offline after first visit
  • ✓ No ads, no tracking, no analytics
  • ✓ Import your own CricClubs scorecards

8 Powerful Features

📊 Dashboard

Instant overview of top batsmen, bowlers, power hitters, death specialists, and recent matches with key metrics.

👥 Players Roster

Browse 100+ players with filters by role, team, and form. See career statistics including runs, wickets, strike rate, and economy.

📈 Trends

Select any player and see their performance over the last 5 matches with visual charts and trend analysis.

🤝 Partnerships

Discover the best batting pairs, see how many times they've played together, and view their average partnership runs.

🏆 Team Split

AI-powered intelligent balancing of 22, 24, or 26 players into two equal-strength teams with fair role distribution.

🎯 XI Builder

Create professional playing XI with smart recommendations for batting order and bowling order based on player stats.

🖨️ Match Card

Generate beautiful, professional lineup cards that are printable and shareable directly to WhatsApp.

🔒 Offline Mode

After opening once, works completely without internet. All data stored on your device. Zero tracking or cloud sync.

How to Get Started
