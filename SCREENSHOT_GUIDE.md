# CricManager - Screenshot Guide for Brochure

**Open the app at:** https://prabhsimransingh.github.io/CricManager/

## Screenshots to Capture (for visual brochure)

### Screenshot 1: Dashboard Tab
- **What to show**: Dashboard page with top players, match statistics
- **How to get**: Click "Dashboard" tab after app loads
- **Key elements visible**:
  - KPI boxes (Total Runs, Wickets, Matches, Players)
  - Top 10 Batsmen table
  - Top 10 Bowlers table
  - Powerplay specialists
  - Recent matches

### Screenshot 2: Players Roster Tab
- **What to show**: Players page with all players and filters
- **How to get**: Click "Players" tab
- **Key elements visible**:
  - Search bar and filter dropdowns
  - List of 100+ players with their stats
  - Role badges (Batsman/Bowler/All-Rounder)
  - Form indicators (Hot 🔥 / Stable / Cold ❄️)

### Screenshot 3: Trends Tab
- **What to show**: Trends page with a player's performance chart
- **How to get**: 
  1. Click "Trends" tab
  2. Select a player from dropdown (e.g., "Prabhleen Singh")
  3. See their last 5 match chart
- **Key elements visible**:
  - Trends dropdown with player names
  - KPI boxes (Career Runs, L5 Avg, Strike Rate)
  - Bar/Line charts showing match-by-match performance
  - Last 5 matches table

### Screenshot 4: Partnerships Tab
- **What to show**: Partnerships page with batting pair analysis
- **How to get**: Click "Partnerships" tab
- **Key elements visible**:
  - Progress bars showing top partnerships
  - Table with partnership details
  - Games played together
  - Average runs per partnership

### Screenshot 5: Team Split Tab
- **What to show**: Team Split with generated balanced teams
- **How to get**:
  1. Click "Team Split" tab
  2. Click "Select 22" button
  3. Click "Generate Split" button
- **Key elements visible**:
  - Two team cards (Team A & Team B)
  - Player count per team (11 each)
  - Role distribution
  - Balance metrics (Total Runs, Strike Rate)
  - Player lists

### Screenshot 6: XI Builder Tab
- **What to show**: XI Builder with 11-player selection
- **How to get**:
  1. Click "XI Builder" tab
  2. Click on 11 player cards to select them
  3. Click "Show XI Card" button
- **Key elements visible**:
  - Player selection cards
  - Selected XI counter (0/11)
  - Generated XI card with batting and bowling order

### Screenshot 7: Match Card Tab
- **What to show**: Professional match card display
- **How to get**:
  1. Click "Match Card" tab
  2. See the XI card in professional format
  3. Show export/print functionality
- **Key elements visible**:
  - Dark green professional card design
  - Team name at top
  - Batting order section
  - Bowling order section
  - Match metadata
  - Export button

## Tips for Screenshots

1. **Load Community Data**: Make sure the 14 matches auto-load (shows "✅ Loaded 14 community scorecard(s)")
2. **Select Players**: For Trends, select a popular player like "Prabhleen Singh" who appears in multiple matches
3. **Clean UI**: Hide browser tabs/address bar (F11 for fullscreen)
4. **Mobile View**: Use browser DevTools to resize to phone dimensions (375x812)
5. **Best Resolution**: Take at 1920x1080 for desktop, then crop as needed
