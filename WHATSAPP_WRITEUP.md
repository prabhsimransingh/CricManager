**Live Demo**: https://prabhsimransingh.github.io/CricManager/

## What is CricManager?

A **FREE, ONLINE cricket match analyzer** that:
- 📊 Tracks player stats & performance
- 📈 Analyzes trends across matches
- 🏆 Builds balanced teams intelligently
- 🎯 Creates professional match cards
- 📱 Works completely offline
- 🔒 Your data stays on YOUR phone

**No signup. No data collection. Just cricket analytics!**

---

## 8 Key Features

### 1️⃣ **Dashboard** 📊
Instant overview of:
- Top batsmen & bowlers
- Power hitters & death specialists
- Recent matches
- Overall player stats (100+ players, 14+ matches)

### 2️⃣ **Players Roster** 👥
Browse all players with:
- Filter by role (Batsman/Bowler/All-Rounder)
- Search by name or team
- Career stats: runs, wickets, strike rate, economy
- Form indicator (Hot 🔥 / Stable / Cold ❄️)

### 3️⃣ **Trends** 📈
Track individual performance:
- Select any player
- See last 5 matches (runs/wickets)
- Visual charts showing performance
- Average runs/wickets trends

### 4️⃣ **Partnerships** 🤝
Discover best batting pairs:
- Batsmen who scored most together
- Games played together
- Average runs per partnership
- Top 15 partnerships ranked

### 5️⃣ **Team Split** 🏆
Auto-balance players intelligently:
- Split 22, 24, or 26 players
- Balanced role distribution
- Fair team strength
- Suggest Team A vs Team B

### 6️⃣ **XI Builder** 🎯
Create optimal playing XI:
- Smart player ordering
- Batting order (by form & strike rate)
- Bowling order (by economy & wickets)
- Display as professional card

### 7️⃣ **Match Card** 🖨️
Professional lineup display:
- Both teams side-by-side
- Print-ready format
- Export to WhatsApp
- Share match preview

### 8️⃣ **Offline Mode** 🔒
- Once opened, works WITHOUT internet
- All data stored on your phone
- No cloud storage
- Complete privacy
